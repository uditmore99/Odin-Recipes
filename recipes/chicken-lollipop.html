Chicken Lollipop

Meat that is luscious, rich, sweet, and zesty. Among the crispy finger-licking chicken appetisers from Indo-Chinese cuisine, this Chicken lollipop is one of the most loved chicken wings starters. It is essentially a recipe for crispy batter-fried chicken wings made using chicken drumettes and wingettes. The inside of these fried chicken wings is juicy and flavorful, while the exterior is superbly crispy. Served with spicy schezwan sauce or sweet chilli garlic dip, its heavenly taste will make you crave it regularly!

  • 500g (6 whole wings) chicken wings
  • 6g (1 tsp) soy sauce
  • 6g (1 tsp) green chilli sauce
  • 15g (1½ tbsp) red chilli sauce
  • 4g salt
  • ¼ tsp MSG (Ajinamoto)
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 2g ginger
  • 1 tbsp egg whites
  • 4 tbsp plain flour (maida)
  • 2 tbsp cornstarch
  • vegetable oil for deep frying
